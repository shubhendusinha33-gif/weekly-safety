Privacy Policy & Data Notice
📍🤳 Location & Camera Notice

Before you continue, please read how this app uses your data.

This app (Weekly Safety Checklist – VMM Central Operations) captures your GPS location and a geo-tagged photo/selfie at the time of login/submission, solely to verify that store/store-visit reviews are completed from the correct location by the correct person.
  • What's collected: GPS coordinates, photo/selfie, timestamp, your E-Code/Store Code.
  • Why: Operational compliance & anti-fraud verification for VMM store reviews.
  • Who can access it: Authorised VMM Central Operations admins only.
  • Retention: Photos are purged on a rolling basis (admin-triggered, typically D-2 and older); text/audit records are retained for compliance reporting.
  • Your rights: You may request access to, or deletion of, your data by contacting Central Operations (see Privacy Policy).
Privacy Policy & Data Notice

App: Weekly Safety Checklist – VMM Central Operations & Business Intelligence

1. Data we collect
GPS coordinates, a photo/selfie captured at login or store-visit submission, device timestamp, your E-Code/Store Code, and submission responses (questions, remarks, attachments).

2. Why we collect it
To verify that the Store representative submitting the Weekly Safety Checklist is physically present at the correct location, and to prevent fraudulent or remote submissions. This is an internal operational compliance control.

3. Sensitive data notice
Your photo/selfie may be treated as sensitive personal data (facial image). It is used only for identity/location verification and is not used for any other purpose such as profiling or facial recognition matching.

4. Who can access it
Only authorised VMM Central Operations & Business Intelligence admin users. Data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) under VMM's account.

5. Retention & deletion
Submission photos are purged on a rolling, admin-triggered basis (typically anything older than the current review window, i.e. D-2 and earlier). Text/audit records (timestamps, GPS, responses) are retained for ongoing compliance and performance reporting in line with internal record-keeping needs.

6. Your rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by writing to your Central Operations admin/contact. Reasonable requests will be actioned, subject to legitimate business/compliance retention needs.

7. Consent
By checking "I agree" and continuing to use this app, you consent to the collection and use of your location and photo as described above. Camera and Location access requests by your browser are used strictly for this purpose and are not recorded or transmitted unless you submit a review.
